Takoa — Privacy Policy

Simple, honest, and to the point.

Last updated: April 2025

The short version: Takoa does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything you log stays on your device and your personal iCloud account. We have no servers. We don't know who you are.

What We Collect

Nothing. Takoa does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, or identifiers of any kind.

We have no backend servers, no user accounts, and no mechanism to receive data from your device.

Your Data Stays on Your Device

All data you create in Takoa — workout history, bodyweight entries, program preferences, and settings — is stored exclusively in two places:

  • Your device — in the local app storage on your iPhone
  • Your iCloud account — if you have iCloud Drive enabled, your data syncs across your own Apple devices via your personal iCloud account

iCloud sync uses Apple's infrastructure directly. Takoa never has access to your iCloud data — it goes from your device to Apple's servers and back to your other devices. Apple's privacy policy governs that relationship.

Apple Health Integration

If you choose to connect Takoa to Apple Health, the app will write workout data (session duration and estimated active calories) to the Health app on your device.

  • This is entirely optional and off by default
  • Data is written directly to Apple's HealthKit on your device
  • Takoa does not transmit any Health data to any server
  • You can disconnect at any time in Settings → Apple Health

Third-Party Services

Takoa does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or data collection services. There are no SDKs in the app that transmit data anywhere.

Advertising

Takoa contains no advertising of any kind. We do not use your data for advertising purposes and we never will.

Children's Privacy

Takoa is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Since we collect no data from anyone, no special handling is required.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control at all times:

  • Delete individual entries from within the app
  • Clear all history in Settings → Data → Clear All History
  • Delete the app entirely to remove all local data

If you use iCloud sync, deleting the app will not automatically remove data from iCloud. You can manage or delete iCloud data through the Settings app on your iPhone under your Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in a meaningful way, we will update the date at the top of this page. Since we collect no data, significant changes are unlikely. Any change that involves data collection would require an app update and explicit user consent.

Health & Safety Disclaimer

Takoa is intended for general fitness and informational purposes only. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider before beginning any new exercise program.
